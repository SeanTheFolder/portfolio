About Me

Executive Leader. Hands-On Builder.

I serve as CISO at Harbors Home Health & Hospice, owning everything most organizations split across three executive roles. Threat detection. Incident response. Board presentations. Vendor contracts. AI governance and adoption. And the full regulatory stack that comes with healthcare security. My environment is Microsoft-first: Sentinel, Defender, Purview, and Intune, plus Copilot and Copilot Studio for automating security workflows, QA, and operations.

Beyond security, I've driven digital transformation from scratch. First HRIS. First CMS. First ticketing system. And a CIS Controls v8 program built from nothing, crosswalked to the HIPAA Security Rule, audited against it, and passed. Third-party penetration tests: passed. The organization operates under a layered US healthcare regulatory stack: HIPAA Security and Privacy Rules, HITECH, OIG compliance guidance for home health agencies and hospices, the Information Blocking Rule under the 21st Century Cures Act, and the 42 CFR Part 2 final rule that reached active enforcement in February 2026. I build things. I'd rather design and deploy a solution myself than hand someone a requirements doc and wait.
