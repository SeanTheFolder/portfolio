Privacy Policy

Plain-English privacy practices for this site.

A CISO's website should practice what it preaches. This page describes what is collected, how it is used, how it is protected, and how to exercise your rights. Last updated: April 2026.

In Plain Language

What Happens When You Visit

This site is a personal portfolio and consulting brochure. It does not handle protected health information (PHI), payment information, or any data covered by HIPAA. The privacy practices below reflect that scope.

Information Collected

What This Site Collects

Information You Provide

When you fill out the contact form or send an email, the following is collected and stored: your name, your company (optional), your email address, and the content of your message. This is used solely to respond to your inquiry. It is not sold, traded, or used for unrelated marketing.

Information Collected Automatically

Standard server logs may capture IP address, browser type, referring page, and the time of visit. This is used for operational purposes (diagnosing issues, understanding traffic patterns) and is retained for 30 days. Aggregated, non-identifying analytics may be collected to understand which pages are useful. No third-party tracking cookies are set.

What Is Not Collected

This site does not collect health information, payment information, social security numbers, or any sensitive personal data. There is no advertising network, no behavioral tracking, and no data broker integration.

How Information Is Used

Use, Sharing, and Retention

Use

Contact form submissions are used to respond to your inquiry and to discuss the engagement you described. They may be retained in email and CRM systems used by the practice for ongoing client relationship management.

Sharing

Information you provide is not shared with third parties for marketing purposes. Vendors that process information on behalf of this site (email provider, CRM provider, hosting provider) operate under standard data processing agreements with appropriate safeguards.

Retention

Server logs are retained for 30 days. Inquiry communications are retained for as long as necessary to maintain the professional relationship or as required by professional standards. You may request deletion at any time.

Your Rights

What You Can Ask For

You have the right to ask what information is held about you, request a copy, request correction, request deletion, or opt out of any future communication. To exercise any of these rights, email sean.welding@email.com with the subject line Privacy Request and indicate what you would like to do.

Reasonable requests will be honored within 30 days. There is no fee for standard requests.

Security

How Information Is Protected

This site is served exclusively over HTTPS with a valid TLS certificate. Backend services holding contact information run under standard enterprise access controls including multi-factor authentication, audit logging, and least-privilege role-based access. Vendor relationships are reviewed for security posture before adoption.

If you observe a security issue with this site, please report it to sean.welding@email.com or via the contact channels listed in /.well-known/security.txt.

Updates

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated when the practices it describes change. Material updates will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Substantial changes that affect existing inquiries will be communicated to affected parties directly.

For questions about this policy, email sean.welding@email.com.
