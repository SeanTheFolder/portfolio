Essay · AI Governance

AI Governance Is Data Governance With Extra Steps

Most AI governance debates skip the unglamorous part: deciding what data the model can see, what prompts it can take, and where its outputs can land. Build that boundary in Purview and Conditional Access first, then build the agent against it. The agent layer is the interface. The boundary is the governance.

Essay · Board Practice

The One-Page Risk Dashboard Rule

The board cannot govern what it cannot understand. Every significant risk gets translated into one of three terms the board already uses: operational continuity, regulatory exposure, reputational cost. The dashboard is one page. The trend is over time. The reading time is under three minutes. Anything else is wasted.

Why Vendor Lock-In on LLMs Is the Strategic Mistake of 2026

The capability differential between Claude, Gemini, GPT, Antigravity, and Copilot is large enough that picking the right model per task produces materially better outcomes. The operational cost of supporting multiple approved models is low if your governance layer is built right.
